Founder and CEO

Huguso was founded in 2015 by Mugisha Mushoka. Mugisha has over 10 years of experience working in Silicon Valley startups (Angaza) and Fortune 100 companies (Microsoft). He has demonstrated expertise in leading IT teams based in Europe, Middle East, Africa and North America. He has successfully delivered 60+ projects in partnership with banks, Mobile Network Operators (MNOs) and other fintech companies.
